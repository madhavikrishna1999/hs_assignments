Two Weigntlifting Players

  • Karanam Malleswari

  • Lalrinnunga

Achievements of Karanam Malleswari

  • She became the National Champion in 1990 and 1991 with 52 kg body weight.

  • Held the National Champion title for 54 kg body weight from 1992 to 1998.

  • Felicitated with the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna award in 1995.

  • Received the civilian award Padma Shri by the Government of India in 1999.

  • Became India's first female weightlifter in 2000 to bring an Olympic medal (bronze).

Achievements of Lalrinnunga

  • In 2016,He won a silver medal at the Youth World Championships. A silver medal at the Asian Youth Championship.

  • In 2017,He won a silver medal at the Youth World Championships and a gold medal at the Commonwealth junior championships.

  • In 2018,He grabbed a bronze medal in the Asian Junior Championships.

  • In 2019,He won a silver medal at the EGAT Cup International weightlifting championship and a gold medal at the Asian Youth Championship.

  • In 2021,He won a gold medal at the Commonwealth Senior Championships.
