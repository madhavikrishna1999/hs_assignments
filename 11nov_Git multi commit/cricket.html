Two Cricket Players

  • M.S.Dhoni

  • Mithali Raj

Achievements of M.S.Dhoni

  • MS Dhoni is the only Indian captain who has led India to all three major ICC events.

  • Under his captaincy, India holds the numero uno position in Test ranking for 18 months.

  • Dhoni is the fourth fastest batsman who has completed 8000 runs in New Zealand fifth and final match.

  • 2008 and 2009 Dhoni was awarded the ICC Player of the Year trophy.

  • Dhoni has won the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna award in 2007.

Achievements of Mithali Raj

  • In 1999, she scored 114 runs (not out) in her One Day International debut against Ireland.

  • In 2005, she led the Indian cricket team to the World Cup finals. Unfortunately, they lost the finals to Australia.

  • Mithali was ranked world no. 1 cricketer in the ODI chart at the Women’s World Cup 2013.

  • She became the first player to score 6000 runs in Women’s ODI in 2017.

  • One of the many records she made, includes playing the most number of WODIs (109) consecutively for a team.
