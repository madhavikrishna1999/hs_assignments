Two Badminton Players

  • Saina Nehwal

  • Pullela Gopichand

Achievements of Saina Nehwal

  • Saina Nehwal is the first-ever badminton player from India to clinch an Olympic medal.

  • she won the bronze medal at the London 2012 Games.

  • Bagged gold medals at the Commonwealth Games of 2018 in women's singles and mixed team events.

  • Received the third highest civilian award 'Padma Bhushan' in 2016.

  • Gold medal in women's singles at the Commonwealth Games, held in New Delhi in 2010.

Achievements of Pullela Gopichand

  • He Represented the nation at three Thomas Cup tournaments.

  • Bagged two gold and one silver medal at the Indian National Games in 1998, held in Imphal.

  • Dominated the National Badminton Championship for straight five times (1996-2000).

  • Honoured with the Dronacharya Award in 2009.

  • Coached the Indian Olympic Badminton Team at the 2016 Rio Olympics.
