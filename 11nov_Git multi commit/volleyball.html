Two Volleyball Players

  • Cristiano Ronaldo

Achievements of Cristiano Ronaldo

  • He has scored a record 817 senior career goals for club and country.

  • He is one of the few recorded players to have made over 1,100 professional career appearances.

  • Overall, Ronaldo has achieved 46 top goalscorer accolades.

  • Ronaldo has won many goal of the competition accolades, and was named runner-up for three nominations.

  • Ronaldo has obtained many other minor achievements, awards and recognitions, by the major sport magazines and newspapers, which are not reported.

Achievements of Ivan Zaytsev

  • In 2009, Zaytsev became the champion of the Mediterranean Games with Italy, as they beat Spain in the final match.

  • Zaytsev has captured 2 medals with the Italy national team at 2 Olympics.

  • He won bronze at the 2012 London Games and silver at the 2016 Rio de Janeiro Games.

  • Italy national team scooped up 2 bronze medals in the World League in 2013 and 2014.

  • With Ivan Zaytsev being a part of the team. They also became the runner-ups in the 2015 World Cup.
